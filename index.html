About the Project

ReliefVision processes pre- and post-disaster satellite images using Structural Similarity Index (SSIM). It detects structural changes, estimates damage severity, and generates prioritized alerts for disaster response.

Problem Statement

Traditional disaster analysis is manual and slow, leading to delayed emergency response and inefficient resource allocation.

Need for System

An automated system is required to quickly detect damage, evaluate severity, and provide actionable insights for disaster management authorities.

Proposed Solution

The system uses SSIM to compare satellite images, classify damage severity, assign priority levels, and generate alerts with recommended actions.
