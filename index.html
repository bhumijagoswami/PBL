About the Project

ReliefVision processes pre- and post-disaster satellite images using SSIM to detect structural changes, estimate damage severity, and generate prioritized alerts.

Problem Statement

Manual disaster analysis is slow and inefficient, leading to delayed response and ineffective resource allocation.

Need for System

Automated systems are required to rapidly analyze satellite imagery, detect damage, and assist in decision-making for faster disaster response.

Proposed Solution

Uses SSIM to compare satellite images, classify damage severity, assign priority levels, and generate actionable alerts.
