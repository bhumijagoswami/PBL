About the Project

ReliefVision is an intelligent disaster assessment platform that leverages Structural Similarity Index (SSIM) and computer vision techniques to analyze satellite imagery and detect structural changes caused by natural disasters.

Problem Statement

Manual disaster impact analysis using satellite imagery is slow and resource-intensive. There is a need for automated systems capable of detecting affected regions and quantifying damage efficiently.

Proposed Solution

ReliefVision compares pre-disaster and post-disaster satellite imagery, applies SSIM-based structural comparison, segments impacted regions, and estimates the percentage of affected area.
